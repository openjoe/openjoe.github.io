Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Lazy Ledger (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using the app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Lazy Ledger, we may collect the following information:

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Account authentication status

1.2 Profile Information

  • Nickname or profile name you set in the app

1.3 User Content

  • Expense and income records you create, including:
  • Category
  • Amount
  • Date
  • Note content

1.4 Feedback Information

  • Feedback content you voluntarily submit through the app

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate your login
  • To store and sync your ledger records
  • To display statistics and summaries based on your records
  • To improve app stability and user experience
  • To review and respond to user feedback

3. Third-Party Services

Lazy Ledger uses Supabase as a third-party backend service provider for account authentication, database storage, and data synchronization.

Your information may be processed or stored through Supabase infrastructure as required for the app’s core functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials safe.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the service.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your account-related data within a reasonable time, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate operational reasons.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Update your profile information
  • Delete your records
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

If you want to request deletion of your account and related data, please contact us through the email address below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Lazy Ledger is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
