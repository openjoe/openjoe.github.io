Delete Your Lazy Ledger Account

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This page explains how users of Lazy Ledger can delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the app:

  1. Open Lazy Ledger
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to the Account page
  4. Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  5. Tap Delete account
  6. Confirm the deletion request

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Your authentication account
  • Your profile information, including nickname and email-linked profile data
  • Your ledger records, including income and expense entries
  • Your submitted feedback records

What Data May Be Retained

We do not intentionally retain your account data after account deletion, except where temporary retention may be required for:

  • technical backup processing
  • security investigation
  • legal or regulatory compliance, if applicable

Any such retained data, if applicable, will only be kept for the minimum period reasonably necessary.
